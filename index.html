At the beginning of 2023, I started my adventure into the world of web development, learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Responsive Web Design, SEO, and WordPress. I've loved every moment of it.

I've always had a passion for the arts. There's something truly magical about the process of transforming a blank canvas into a work of art. Recently, I ventured into digital illustration, and it's been a fascinating experience.

My artistic background significantly influenced my choice to study front-end development. I'm passionate about crafting digital experiences and bringing visual concepts to life. Coding is an endless, exciting game that constantly presents new challenges.

Front-end development combines my artistic sensibilities with technical skills, letting me create elements that spring to life in a browser. It’s like painting a chef-d'oeuvre but with pixels and lines of code!
